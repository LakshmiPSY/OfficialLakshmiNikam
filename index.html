About Dr. Nikam

Dr. Lakshmi Nikam works with adolescents, adults, and older adults, offering care that is confidential, holistic, and grounded in evidence.

Her approach brings together medication, psychotherapy, and lifestyle interventions — tailored to each person, not a one-size-fits-all plan — to help individuals achieve lasting emotional well-being.

Consultations are available both online and in person, so support is never far out of reach.
